Daegeun Kim

Daegeun Kim is a computational designer and data scientist currently pursuing an M.S. in Computational Design Practices at Columbia University. His work focuses on data-driven, geospatial, and machine learning based analysis for urban and real estate decision-making.

He develops computational systems that translate complex spatial and urban data into actionable insights, bridging design thinking with analytics, modeling, and automation in the built environment.

He also practices urban and landscape photography.
